Fly Tone Asia

I spent total of 12 months with Fly Tone Asia. Firstly, I started as an intern, and then I collaborated with them on more projects as a freelancer. My job scope in the company was mostly on app programming using Unity (C#), level designing and 3D modelling. Due to time constraints, we don't always work on 3D models from scratch. Instead we purchase assets from Turbosquid or other platforms and rework on the model, or at some situations, we are demanded to make the 3D model from scratch.

Across this 12 months, we had gone through a lot of projects for clients such as Celcom, Toyota, Avisena and so on.

Infra Sarawak Launching
INFRA, or also known as Institut Kemajuan Desa in Sarawak conducted their launching. A project collabrated with FlyTone Productions, to prepare a Leap Motion used application, for a Infrastructure/Campus launching. The project was fully covered by Malaysian Government, from the budget to all other expenses.

The main VIP for the launching is Datuk Seri Rina binti Mohd Harun, Minister of Women, Family and Community Development.

We worked in a group of 2, in completing this entire application, from scratch. It was a project that was given in a week due, and the pressure was real until the launching. There were many changes had to be done time to time based on client's request.

My Role:
My role in the projects was to work on the modelling of the Hornbill and its animation. Apart from that, I also worked on the function of the Leap Motion device to launch the bird as per in the video.
