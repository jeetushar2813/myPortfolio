VirtuAR: Transforming Ideas into Augmented Reality

VirtuAR stands at the forefront of innovation, introducing an immersive Augmented Reality experience tailored exclusively for Apple devices. Our application, designed for both LiDAR and non-LiDAR supported devices, redefines visualization by seamlessly merging the virtual and physical worlds.

Key Features:
- Immersive AR Experience: Dive into a world where your designs come to life in stunning 3D, creating unparalleled client engagement.
- Personal 3D Model Repository: Store and manage your 3D models effortlessly, ensuring easy access and seamless integration into your projects.
- Subscription-Based Access: Enjoy the benefits of our feature-rich application through a convenient subscription model, tailored to meet your needs.

My Role:
